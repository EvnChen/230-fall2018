Preserving Software Settings Across Lab Machines

It can be frustrating to spend time configuring software on your lab computer and then lose those settings when you move to another machine. Unfortunately, it's difficult to configure software to always use your network drive for settings. Rather than trying to remember every setting every time, there are some ways you can store your settings files on a network drive, and then quickly apply them when you log into a new machine. Below are some approaches for doing that with Visual Studio Code and with Git.
